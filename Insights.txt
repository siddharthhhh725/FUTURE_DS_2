# Customer Churn & Retention Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn and retention patterns using the Telco Customer Churn dataset. The objective is to identify factors influencing customer attrition, evaluate retention trends, and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer loyalty and business growth.

## Business Problem
Customer churn directly impacts revenue and profitability. Understanding why customers leave and identifying high-risk customer segments helps businesses develop effective retention strategies.

## Objectives
- Analyze customer churn rates and retention trends.
- Identify customer segments with high churn risk.
- Examine customer lifetime and tenure patterns.
- Discover key factors contributing to churn.
- Provide recommendations to improve customer retention.

## Key Insights
- Customers on month-to-month contracts exhibit the highest churn rates.
- Churn is significantly higher among customers with tenure below six months.
- Electronic check payment users show elevated churn risk.
- Long-term contract customers demonstrate stronger retention.
- Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn probability.

## Recommendations
- Improve onboarding experience during the first six months.
- Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts.
- Implement proactive retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
- Enhance payment experience and reduce billing friction.
- Introduce loyalty and reward programs for long-tenure customers.
