Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn using the Telco Customer Churn dataset. The goal is to identify retention opportunities and understand the factors driving customer attrition.

Business Objectives

Identify Churn Drivers
Analyze Customer Segments
Measure Retention Performance
Improve Customer Lifetime Value

Dataset Information

  • 7043 Customers
  • 21 Variables
  • Subscription Business Data
  • Source: Kaggle

Dashboard Gallery

Key Insights

Month-to-month customers show the highest churn rates.
Customers below six months tenure are at greatest risk.
Electronic check users demonstrate elevated churn.
Long-term contracts significantly improve retention.

Recommendations

Improve onboarding programs.
Promote annual plans.
Launch retention campaigns.
Reward loyal customers.
